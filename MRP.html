POND Brain

Master's Research Project

This project aims to address a communication gap between the scientists who study the neurobiological underpinnings of neurodevelopmental disorders and those who are personally affected by the disorders.

It's a web-based application that connects an interactive 3D brain model with descriptions of the structure and function of the brain.

As the sole designer and lead developer of the project, I was responsible for everything from defining the scope of the project to creating final project assets.

Tools

HTML/CSS/Js (Three.js) - 3D Slicer - ZBrush - Photoshop - Illustrator

Funding

Ontario Brain Institute - Province of Ontario Neurodevelopmental Disorders Network - Vesalius Trust

Awards

Audience

Parents of children with neurodevelopmental disorders
