UX RESEARCH A/B TESTING UX/UI

Optimizing an overlooked onboarding flow to increase first-time creation by 17%

The context

Users come to Venngage for graphic design templates

Venngage is a web-based SaaS graphic design tool. Many of Venngage’s core users use the tool to create documents like infographics, reports, presentations, and diagrams to aid communication at work.

Venngage’s primary acquisition channel is organic search. Most users come to us looking for a tool (like a “report maker”) or a template for a specific use case (like an “annual report template”).

The problem

Significant drop-off early in the user journey

🤔 The creation rate for new users was sitting around 60%, meaning that ~40% of all users who sign up never create a design

The process

😕 “I clicked on a product roadmap and now I am being presented with options that are not roadmaps. How do I go back to that selection? I don't seem to have that option. It says "pick up to three" and the options do not include roadmap. I am setting up my profile on the site, I think”

🤷 “I was looking for the template I saw online, now I can't find it. It was a project plan. That's the one, but I have to sign up first. And it's not giving me that option as it's asking me to pick templates.”

😠 “I am trying to create a school news bulletin. However the choices are not the templates I am trying to use. When I pick a template, it brings me back to a "let's customize" page which only gives me a few options none of which are the school news bulletin. It lists resumes, mind maps etc”

The solution

The results

📈 Creation for users that signed up from a paid template increased by 17%, from 58% to 68%.

📈 Within this flow, creation from Recommended templates increased by 157%, from 21% to 54%.
