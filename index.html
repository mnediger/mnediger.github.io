About me

I’m a product designer based in Toronto.

I love being involved in every part of end-to-end product design. I’m happiest when I’m talking to customers, jamming on solutions with my team, and geeking out over product strategy.

With a background in Biomedical Communications and a passion for data visualization, I aim to use the principles of visual communication to craft clean, clear, accessible designs.

Outside of work you can find me exploring the city by bike, reading, gaming, or making something (usually out of flour, wood, or clay).
