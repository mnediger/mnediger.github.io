The Buzz on Caffeine

Mock Journal Cover

The goal of this project was to create a visually engaging scene that could be used as the cover image of a scientific magazine. This editorial illustration references a study by Couvillon et al. (2015) that suggests caffeine-containing nectar may influence bees' foraging behaviour (find a summary of their research here).
