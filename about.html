I'm Midori.

I design solutions to communication problems in science and medicine.

These solutions can take on a variety of forms - from an interactive web app that helps medical students learn human anatomy, to a print brochure that teaches a patient about a medical procedure. The defining characteristic of all of my solutions - they distill complex scientific concepts to their essence, and present them in ways that are equally engaging and beautiful.

I've had training in science, art, and design. I recently completed my Master of Science in Biomedical Communications at the University of Toronto, with a focus on Media Design.

If you're interested in collaborating, send me a message and you'll hear from me shortly!
